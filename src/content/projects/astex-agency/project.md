# ASTEX — AI Automation Agency

Founded and leading an AI automation agency in Dubai, helping businesses leverage artificial intelligence to optimize operations, build products, and scale.

## Services

### MVP Development
Building AI-powered products from concept to launch in 2-4 weeks. From idea validation to working prototype.

### Process Automation
Identifying bottlenecks in business workflows and implementing AI solutions that save hours of manual work.

### Voice Agents
Deploying conversational AI for customer support, sales, and appointment scheduling.

### AI Chatbots
Custom chatbot solutions powered by RAG, LLMs, and domain-specific knowledge bases.

## How We Work

1. **Discovery** (1 week) — Understand the business, identify high-impact opportunities
2. **Prototype** (1-2 weeks) — Build working MVP with core AI functionality
3. **Launch** (1 week) — Deploy, integrate with existing systems, train the team
4. **Iterate** (ongoing) — Monitor, optimize, and expand capabilities

## Technologies

Python, LangChain, OpenAI API, Anthropic Claude, FastAPI, React, Docker, Twilio, Vapi
