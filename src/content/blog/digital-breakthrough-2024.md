Secured 2nd place at Digital Breakthrough — one of Russia's most prestigious hackathons with thousands of participants.

## The challenge

Build a system that can accurately classify video content by combining multiple modalities — visual frames, audio features, and text metadata.

## Our approach

Developed a multimodal pipeline that fuses visual embeddings from video frames with audio feature extraction, creating a unified representation for classification. The system achieved high accuracy across diverse content categories.
