The most underrated app in the field of AI agents.

On the weekend, I discovered the Docker MCP Toolkit and haven't stopped using it since then.

1. 312 integrations with various MCPs
2. Connects to Claude Desktop/Code in one click
3. Simple and secure
4. Free

Personally, I really enjoy using Claude in conjunction with Notion, which allows me to keep the apps in the same context.

Number 2 — YouTube transcripts — you just send a link to the video and you can work on the analysis for as long as you want, saving hours of time.

I highly recommend it to everyone, as I'm really enjoying it myself.
