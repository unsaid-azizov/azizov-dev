Got featured by [@tellsef](https://www.instagram.com/tellsef/) in a career spotlight covering my journey in AI.

## The story they told

**From school hackathons to Senior AI Engineer in 1.5 years.**

The post covers five chapters of my career:

### 1. The beginning
Got into machine learning in 10th grade in Kaspiysk, Dagestan. Won about 10 competitions including NTO and silver in a Google Kaggle challenge. Became the absolute winner of [AIIJC](/blog/aiijc-2021/) — Russia's largest AI competition — and presented the project to government leaders on national television.

### 2. Corporate experience & open source
- **SberAI**: Co-authored Kandinsky 2.2, 3.0, 3.1 — Russia's flagship generative AI models. Built an auto-presentation system using GigaChat + Kandinsky.
- **XLabs AI (Senior ML Engineer)**: Built the [X-FLUX ecosystem](/projects/x-flux/project/) — 4,000+ stars on GitHub, nearly 1 million downloads on HuggingFace.

### 3. Social impact
AI for cultural preservation: the [Lezgin language project](/blog/lezgin-translator-2-2026/). Not just coding for corporations — building meaningful social projects. Together with 1,000+ volunteers, we launched a translator, trained a TTS model, and wrote a Telegram bot for data collection. Everything open-source.

### 4. Going international
Founded an AI agency with clients from Moscow, Dubai, and London — from industrial enterprises to content farms. A subscriber from Twitter (X) invited us to an exclusive business club, where expertise and hunger for results sealed the deal.

### 5. The takeaway
Technical hunger + real ROI beats empty AI hype. Every day with burning eyes into new challenges.
