Placed 58th out of 1,000+ teams (top 5%) in the Google — Isolated Sign Language Recognition Kaggle competition, earning a silver medal.

## The challenge

Google tasked participants with building a model to classify isolated American Sign Language signs from video sequences. The competition attracted over a thousand teams from around the world.

## Approach

Built a multi-class classification model combining temporal and spatial features from video frames. Optimized inference for TensorFlow Lite to run on mobile devices — matching Google's goal of making sign language recognition accessible on everyday hardware.

## Why it matters

This was my second major project in sign language AI (after [AIIJC](/blog/aiijc-2021/)). Competing against seasoned Kaggle grandmasters and placing in the top 5% validated that the skills I was building were world-class.
